Research Projects

Geometry-Aware Terrain Reconstruction (2026)

  • Pipeline for high-resolution 3D terrain surfaces from UAV photogrammetry data.
  • Integrates geometry processing and deep learning for smoothing and feature extraction.
  • Uses mesh representations, point-cloud processing, and geometric descriptors for risk analysis.
  • Includes curvature estimation, Laplacian smoothing, and mesh optimization.
  • Tools: Open3D, PyVista, PyMeshLab; discrete differential geometry study.

LightPoseNet (2025)

  • Lightweight CNN framework for WiFi-based 3D human pose estimation.
  • Baseline and SE-attention variants reached 40 mm MPJPE and 93.31% PCK@0.10.
  • 300x parameter reduction for real-time edge deployment.
  • Privacy-preserving through-wall sensing with commodity WiFi.

Radar-Based UAV Classification (2025)

  • Deep learning pipeline for drone vs bird classification.
  • Fine-tuned EfficientNetB3 to 97.10% classification accuracy.
  • Used DIAT-uSAT micro-Doppler dataset.

AI-Powered Disaster Prediction (2024)

  • ML pipeline combining satellite imagery and environmental data.
  • GIS features for spatio-temporal flood and landslide forecasting.
  • Presented at IIT Kanpur disaster technology showcase.
